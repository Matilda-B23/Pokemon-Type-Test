Welcome to my quiz!

This is a test playground for me to learn while taking classes on web development.

This is a quiz I've designed to test knowledge of Pokemon type matchups.

You have 4 options of effectiveness:

  • No effect (0.0)
  • Not very effective (0.5)
  • Neutrally effective (1.0)
  • Super effective (2.0)

Each question will present the attacking type, and defending type.
Your job is to determine how effective the attacking type is against the defending type.

There are 8 total questions. Have fun!
